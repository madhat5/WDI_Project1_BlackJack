Welcome to The Virtual Sands

Your only competition is The Dealer.

The aim of the game is to accumulate a higher point total than the dealer, but without going over 21.

The cards 2 through 10 have their face valgitue, J, Q, and K are worth 10 points each, and the Ace is worth either 1 or 11 points.
